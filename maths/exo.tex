\underline{Exercice 11 :}

\bigskip
\bigskip
\bigskip

Soit $u\in\C$ et $z\in\C \setminus \R$. On pose :

\begin{align}
\begin{split}
\frac{z-u\overline{z}}{1-u}\in\R
\Longleftrightarrow \frac{z-u\overline{z}}{1-u} = \overline{\Bigl(\frac{z-u\overline{z}}{1-u}\Bigl)}&\\
&= \frac{\overline{z-u\overline{z}}}{\overline{1-u}} = \frac{\overline{z}-\overline{u}z}{1-u}\\
~\\
&\Longleftrightarrow \frac{z-\overline{u}z}{1-u} = \frac{\overline{z}-\overline{u}z}{1-\overline{u}}\\
\end{split}
\end{align}
